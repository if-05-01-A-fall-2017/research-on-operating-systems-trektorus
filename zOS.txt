z/OS is a 64-bit-os for IBM mainframes produced by IBM. 
z/OS supports stable mainframe systems and standards such 
as CICS, COBOL, IMS, DB2, RACF, SNA, WebSphere MQ, record-oriented
data access methods, REXX, CLIST, SMP/E, JCL, TSO/E, and
ISPF, among others. However, z/OS also supports 64-bit Java, C, C++, and
